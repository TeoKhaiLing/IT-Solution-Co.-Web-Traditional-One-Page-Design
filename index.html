About Us

Treal Tech, is a FinTech start-up in technology-enabled, multi-tier customer trading and management solution for Financial Services industry. We leverage the latest technology offer in high volume upstream connectivity, customer focused UI and insight driven management tools; creating new competitive edge for our clients and enlighten their customer journey. We are a young, fun, energetic and rapidly growing team of 20 or so, operating in Malaysia, Singapore as well as Shanghai for our clients operating in Asia cities, Australia and Europe.

Our Belief

We believe in attracting only the best talent in their respective field of expertise and passion, providing the best working environment, foster a cohesive team and handsomely reward our best talent.

We compromise for nothing but being excellence in everything we do and in so doing create value for our employees, customers and stakeholders.

Treal Tech practises the inclusive culture and is unbiased towards gender, race, religion and physical ability.
